About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an action-packed survival game where you face endless waves of increasingly difficult monsters. As you progress, you'll unlock powerful weapons, abilities, and upgrades to extend your survival time.

With its roguelike elements and procedurally generated encounters, every playthrough offers a unique experience. How long can you survive against the monster hordes?

Game Highlights

  • ✓ Intense survival gameplay with endless progression
  • ✓ Unlock and upgrade various weapons and abilities
  • ✓ Fight dozens of unique monster types
  • ✓ Simple controls but deep strategic gameplay

How to Play

Monster Survivors features intuitive controls designed for both desktop and mobile play:

On Desktop:

  • WASD or Arrow Keys: Move your character
  • Mouse: Aim weapons and abilities
  • Space: Use special ability when charged

On Mobile:

  • Left Joystick: Move your character
  • Right Joystick: Aim weapons and abilities
  • Special Button: Activate special ability

Game Features

Procedural Generation

Every playthrough is unique with procedurally generated maps, monster spawns, and power-up drops, ensuring replayability and fresh challenges.

Weapon Evolution

Combine weapons and passive items to evolve your arsenal into more powerful forms, unlocking devastating attacks and area-of-effect abilities.

Character Progression

Level up during gameplay to increase stats and unlock new abilities. Permanent upgrades between runs allow for long-term progression.

Monster Types

Slimes

Fast but weak monsters that attack in groups

Skeletons

Ranged attackers that fire projectiles

Golems

Slow but powerful tanks with high health

Boss Monsters

Special enemies that appear at time intervals
